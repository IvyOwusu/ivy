Miss Treasurel Adepa Kissi-Amakye

At Enchanted Essence, the love for perfumes is the driving force behind the business. Founded by Miss Treasurel , a student from the University of Ghana, this journey began with advertising for others and earning commissions. Over a year and a few months, this passion has evolved into a dedicated endeavor to bring exquisite scents to customers. Each perfume in the collection is carefully selected to ensure everyone finds their perfect match. Join Enchanted Essence on this aromatic adventure and experience the magic of curated fragrances. Treat yourself and let Enchanted Essence make every moment special. Discover the passion in every bottle!
